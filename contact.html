Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our consultation process.

What should I bring to my consultation?

Please bring the garments you wish to have altered, along with any shoes or undergarments you plan to wear with them. This helps us ensure the perfect fit.

How long does a consultation typically take?

Initial consultations generally last 30-45 minutes, depending on the number of garments and complexity of the alterations needed.

Is there a fee for consultations?

Initial consultations are complimentary. If you proceed with alterations, a 50% deposit is required to begin work.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

While we prefer in-person consultations for the most accurate assessments, we do offer virtual pre-consultations for clients with special circumstances. Please contact us to discuss your needs.
